Welcome to SLoC! In order to complete this game you must venture forth through the code, eliminating bugs as you go. There are exploits available to you on your journey that will warp you to different points of the code, as well as instructor assitance that you can pick up to tackle more bugs.

You are blue, your mission is to navigate through the layers of code prepping each one for release.
Purple: Code Exploits, these teleport you to various spots of code
Red: These are malicious bugs that live in your code, you can only defeat so many before you need instructor assistance
LightBlue: Instructor Assistance, you can pick up instructor assistance to give you the extra push you need to eliminate more bugs
Green: This is the goal that you are trying to reach for the level
