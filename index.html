Welcome to Seven Levels of Code

Coder's Morale:

Floor: 1

Welcome to SLoC! That little silver person is you, and it's your job to defeat the bugs and clear the seventh level for release.
Those Question marks are the bugs, you can defeat them by running into them twice, but beware! Each time you hurt them, they chip away at your "Coder's Morale"
If your Coder's Morale drops to zero you lose the game and the project never reaches production. Not to fret though!
Those keys that you see are instances of Instructor Aid, each one you pick up give you +5 morale.
Like all good Coders, you are good at using exploits, the clear looking tiles will teleport you to random points in the code.
Your primary goal is to reach the staircase leading down to the next level.
Goodluck and Godspeed!
